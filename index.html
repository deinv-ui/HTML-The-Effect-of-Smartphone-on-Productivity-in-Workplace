Introduction

Since the first release of the iPhone on June 29th 2007, Steve Jobs had introduced the concept of smartphone to the public. 15 years have passed since the introduction of smartphones, it has already become a part of our everyday life and widely reshaped our habits.

The definition of smartphone is a portable device that can make phone calls and send text messages, have access to cellular internet, and have more advanced features that can perform tasks like a computer. The function of smartphones is so versatile nowadays and covers every aspect of life.

From daily communications via phone calls, text messages, video call; to entertainment like gaming, video streaming, social media; or some more practical use like maps and navigation, e-wallet, online shopping, food deliveries, e-hailing services and more. Smartphones have become a convenient tool for our modern life and it is hard to live without smartphones, the covid-19 pandemic also has proven the importance of smartphones to us as everything goes virtual. As smartphones are becoming a part of our life, we need to closely inspect the effect that smartphones can bring to us.
