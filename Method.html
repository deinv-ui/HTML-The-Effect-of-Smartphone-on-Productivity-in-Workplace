Method


For this research we have prepared a questionnaire to obtain data from the public. The goal of the research is to explore the impacts of smartphones on workplace productivity in Malaysia. See the form here



Questions:

  1. What is your gender?
  2. What is your age?
  3. What is your current status? (work or student)
  4. How many years since you started using a smartphone?
  5. How many hours do you use smartphones per day?
  6. What is your most frequent application type? (multiple choice: video streaming / social media / video games / communication / work or study)
  7. How much time do you use smartphones during work?
  8. Do smartphones affect your productivity?
  9. Do you think smartphones are essential for your work?
  10. What is your opinion on BYOD policies



Details of the form:

The number of questions in the form is kept at 10, to ease the respondent in filling the form. Question 1, 2 & 3 is to gather demographic information of the respondent. The information gathered is indirect / non-personal to protect the privacy of the respondent. Question 3 to 8 is to gather information about smartphone usage of the respondent. Question 9 & 10 is to gather thoughts and opinions of the respondent about the topic of smartphones in the workplace.
