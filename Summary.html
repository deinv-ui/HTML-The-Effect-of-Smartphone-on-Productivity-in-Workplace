Overall Summary

In the survey, most of the people (59.2%) states that smartphones affect their productivity, while the other part (40.8%) states that smartphones do not affect their productivity. Additionally, all of the people (100%) state that smartphones are essential to their work. Besides, 42.7% of people use smartphones for 8 and more hours daily, there are also 33% of people using smartphones for 5 to 6 hours daily. Furthermore, 57.3% of people use smartphones during work for 3 hours and above, and 35% of the people use smartphones during work for 1 hours and below. In short, smartphones do actually affect productivity negatively, but smartphones have become an essential during work. A complete ban on smartphones is not practical, because smartphones have become an essential tool nowadays, but some regulations should be introduced to prevent misuse of smartphones during work.
