Abstract



Smartphones have become a versatile and popular tool in this era of information, literally everyone has at least one smartphone in their hands. Smartphones allow easier communications between people and make information / knowledge available to the public. While many researchers claim that smartphones can have negative impacts on work efficiency, lowering productivity, contributing to mental problems and more.

This study investigates the effect of smartphones on productivity. 103 people have participated in this online survey. The smartphone usage time and pattern are examined in the context of participants age and their self perceived productivity.

keywords: effect of smartphones, productivity, online survey
